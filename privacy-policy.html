PixSort — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 July 2026 · Contact: support@pixsort.ai

Short version: PixSort collects nothing about you. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking. Your photos and videos stay on your device (and your own iCloud). Nothing is ever uploaded to us or shared with third parties.

Information we collect

None. PixSort has no user accounts and no servers that receive your data. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal information, photos, videos, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, or diagnostics.

Your photos and videos

  • With your permission, PixSort reads your Apple Photos library to import copies into its own private storage on your device.
  • PixSort only ever reads from Apple Photos. It never deletes, moves, or modifies anything in the Photos app — your originals are untouched.
  • Imported copies are stored locally in the app's private area on your device. If you use iCloud, they may be included in your own device/iCloud backups. They are never sent to us.

On-device processing

All features — including AI search, image labelling, text recognition, tagging, and duplicate detection — run entirely on your device. Your photos are never sent to any server for processing.

The one network request

When a photo contains GPS coordinates, PixSort may send those coordinates only (never the photo) to Apple's mapping service to show a place name, exactly as the Apple Maps and Photos apps do. No photo, video, or personal identifier is included.

Exporting and sharing

When you choose to export or share, PixSort hands your selected files to Apple's standard system share sheet or document picker. Where the files go — AirDrop, Messages, iCloud Drive, a USB drive, or a third‑party provider you have installed — is entirely your choice, and is governed by that destination's own privacy terms.

Children's privacy

PixSort does not collect any data from anyone, including children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, we will update this page and revise the date above.
